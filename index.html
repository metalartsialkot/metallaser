About Metal Art Sialkot

Welcome to Metal Art Sialkot, your destination for premium Islamic wall art crafted with precision metal laser cutting technology.

Our journey began with a simple vision: to bring the timeless beauty of Islamic calligraphy into modern homes through high-quality stainless steel craftsmanship. Every piece we create is designed to inspire faith, elegance, and sophistication while enhancing the beauty of your living space.

We specialize in luxury Islamic wall décor made from premium 0.8 mm stainless steel with an exclusive golden finish. Using advanced laser cutting technology, we transform meaningful Islamic verses into stunning works of art.

Premium Islamic Metal Wall Art

Our Golden Stainless Steel Calligraphy collection features timeless Islamic verses including Ayat ul Kursi, First Kalma, Bismillah Hir Rehman Ir Raheem, Hasbunallah, Masha Allah La Quwata Illa Billah, and many more. Each piece is crafted from premium 0.8 mm stainless steel with an exclusive golden mirror finish. Using advanced laser cutting technology, we create intricate designs that combine spiritual significance with contemporary elegance. Perfect for living rooms, entrances, prayer rooms, offices, and mosques. Rust resistant, easy to install, and made with care in Sialkot, Pakistan.
